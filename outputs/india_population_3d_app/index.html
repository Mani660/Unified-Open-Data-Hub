Accuracy Notes

2011 population, density, sex ratio, rural/urban and gender values are taken from the supplied workbook. The 2024 values are treated as estimates because they are not Census 2011 fields. Rural/urban and gender percentages are calculated from their classified totals, so the pie percentages stay internally consistent even if category totals differ slightly from the national total row.
